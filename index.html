Projects

FastRTC Kroko

Created an extension for FastRTC, a real-time communication library for Python. Adds the Kroko-ASR model to the library, which is a state-of-the-art automatic speech recognition model.

Immersive Camera

A next-gen update (and rework) of the popular Immersive Camera mod, which overhauls much of the camera system in The Witcher 3. It is written in WitcherScript, a derivative of ActionScript 3. Fully compatible for version 4.04+ and includes the optional Immersive Meditation as well.

We're Ducked!

A solo or co-op game where you play as a duck trying to escape a rapidly sinking island. The island is known for it's diverse weather, and you must hunt for resources and shelter to survive until rescue arrives.

Scitech Vex AI

An AI-powered robotics pipeline developed for the 2025 VEX AI Competition, focused on enabling fully autonomous gameplay. At its core, the system integrates deep learning-based computer vision with strategic motion planning to allow a robot to perceive its environment, identify high-value targets, and navigate the field without human intervention.
